Baked Whole Halloumi

£12

This elegant appetizer or light meal combines the best sweet and salty flavors. A whole block of Cypriot halloumi is baked until charred and gooey. The halloumi oozes and melts into jammy apricots, creating a delightful combination. Drizzled with honey and a touch of harissa for a hint of spice, it’s perfect served with fresh bread or creamy hummus. A true all-in-one cheese board!

Lamb Kofta Skewers

£15

These Middle Eastern lamb meatball kebabs are a true culinary gem. Ground lamb is gently spiced with cinnamon, coriander, and cumin, then mixed with finely minced parsley and fresh mint. The result? Juicy, flavorful skewers that are perfectly grilled. Served on a bed of couscous with a side of tabbouleh, these kofta skewers offer something refreshingly different. Don’t miss out on this Mediterranean delight!

Seafood Paella

£14

This vibrant and aromatic Spanish dish has captivated taste buds worldwide. Originating from the coastal regions of Spain, it’s a symphony of flavors and textures. Picture saffron-infused rice studded with a medley of seafood: plump mussels, succulent shrimp, and tender squid. The rice forms a golden crust at the bottom of the pan, adding depth to each bite. Squeeze fresh lemon wedges over the paella, sprinkle with parsley, and savor the taste of the sunny shores of the Mediterranean!.
