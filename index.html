Why Canine Coffee House?

Delicious Treats

For the humans, we serve up locally sourced home cooked dishes, as well as plenty of healthy options, including gluten-free, vegetarian, and vegan dishes. Whether you’re craving a hearty meal or a light snack, we’ve got you covered. But wait, there’s more! Our special doggy menu features treats that’ll make your pup’s tail wag with joy. From Puppuccinos (a big bowl of goat milk with chunks of black pudding) to wholesome nibbles, we’ve curated a selection that’ll have your furry friend begging for more. So, whether you’re sipping on a latte or sharing a snack with your canine companion, Paws For Coffee is the place where food, friendship, and furry love come together.

That tail wagging feeling

We believe that dogs are more than just furry friends—they’re healers for the soul. Our cozy space is designed to be a haven for both humans and their canine companions. Why do we say that? Well, science tells us that spending time with dogs can significantly boost our mental health. So, while you enjoy our scrumptious bites or sip on a Canineccino, remember that your mental health matters—and so does your pup’s. Whether you’re seeking solace, laughter, or simply a warm spot to cuddle with your dog, Canine Coffee is where tails wag, hearts connect, and well-being flourishes.

We're barking mad

Whether it is a butt sniff or a handshake, We’re more than just a place to grab your favorite brew. We’re a hub for connection, conversation, and community. Our comfortable seating arrangements invite you to linger, chat with friends, or strike up a conversation with fellow coffee enthusiasts and dog lovers. Whether you’re here to work, eat, or simply enjoy a cup of coffee, you’ll find that our Canine Coffee is a warm and welcoming space where relationships brew over shared love for that perfect cup and our furry friends.
